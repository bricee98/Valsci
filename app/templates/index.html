How to Use Valsci

Valsci helps you conduct automated literature reviews by analyzing scientific claims. Follow these simple steps:

1. Add Your Claims

You can add claims in two ways:

  • Type individual claims into the text box below
  • Upload a .txt file containing multiple claims (one per line)

2. Enhance Your Claims Optional

Use our AI-powered enhancement feature to rephrase your claims for optimal searchability.

3. Configure Processing Options

Choose your preferred review type:

  • Full Review: Comprehensive analysis using complete paper texts
  • Abstracts Only: Quick analysis using paper abstracts
  • LLM Screen: Rapid AI-powered screening

4. Submit and Track

Submit your batch for processing. Add your email to receive notifications when your results are ready.
